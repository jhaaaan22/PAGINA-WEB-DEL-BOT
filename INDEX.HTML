Privacy Policy

1. Overview

Please read the privacy policy below to learn more about how we collect, use, and share your personal information.

2. Data we Collect

Lara-bot doesn't store your username or profile picture. We don't store any data about users. We only store information about guilds, such as the prefixes, your language, and other parameters. You can ask to delete your data by contacting me, the bot owner, DJHammer#7246, or by joining the support server at https://discord.gg/Wx9JAujzn3.

3. Bot rules

By using Lara-bot, you agree to the following rules:

  1. No spamming. Spamming is defined as executing lots of commands without actually using the bot.
  2. No advertising.
  3. No hacking. We do not allow any hacking. If you find a bug, please contact me as the bot owner, DJHammer#7246, or join the support server at https://discord.gg/Wx9JAujzn3.

4. Sharing your data

We do not share your personal data in any way, primarily because we do not store it.

5. Content

Lara-bot streams content from SoundCloud/Spotify only. When you send a Spotify link, the bot will search for the song on SoundCloud/Spotify and play it.

Final

If you have any questions or concerns, you can reach us on Discord using this server: https://discord.gg/Wx9JAujzn3 or contact me as the bot owner, DJHammer#7246.
